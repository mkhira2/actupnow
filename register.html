HOW TO REGISTER

1. Simply decide on if you are coming alone or with a business partner/spouse or vacationing spouse.

2. Decide if you want a room all to yoruself (upgrade).

3. Send an email to David@DavidHira.com specifying the session you want and the details of who is coming.

4. Your 50% deposit saves your space! The balance is due no later than 45 days prior to the first day of your session.

5. Payment: A PayPal invoice will be sent to you OR send a check to David Hira Productions, P.O. Box 1445, Cedar Hill, Texas 75106 once you receive confirmation that there is space for you.

6. See our cancellation policy at the bottom of the page.


WHAT IS INCLUDED

Once you land at SJO airport in San Jose, Costa Rica, (you are responsible for your airfare) you won't need to spend another dime! We'll pick you up/drop you off at and resort. All meals, snacks and beverages are included (one wine/beer at each dinner) as well as your double bed in a shared room at the resort. We include all excursions (including transportation and admission charges) and even some goodies along the way.
